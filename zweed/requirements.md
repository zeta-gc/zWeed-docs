## Supported Systems

You need **one** from each category:

### Inventory (required)
- ✅ ox_inventory (fully supported)
- 🟡 qb-inventory (stub included — implement your API calls)
- 🔧 Custom (template provided)

### Target (required)
- ✅ ox_target (fully supported)
- 🟡 qb-target (stub included)
- 🔧 Custom (template provided)

### Notifications (required)
- ✅ ox_lib (fully supported)
- ✅ qb-core (fully supported)
- ✅ okokNotify (fully supported)
- 🔧 Custom (template provided)

### Framework (required)
- ✅ ESX (fully supported)
- 🟡 QBCore (stub included)
- ✅ Standalone (no framework needed, uses ace permissions)

### Skills (optional)
- ✅ None (default — no skill tree)
- ✅ peakville_skills (fully supported)
- 🔧 Custom (template provided)
